About

Momma Cusses: A Field Guide to Responsive Parenting & Trying Not to Be the Reason Your Kid Needs Therapy. This is a book about parenting and outlines the differences between responsive parenting and reactive parenting, from the author's point of view.

Author

Gwenna Laithland started Momma Cusses in 2019 first as a e-zine and shifting toward a social media focus in 2020. She still lives in Oklahoma with her husband and all three kids.
